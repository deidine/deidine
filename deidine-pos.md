# Privacy Policy

## POS Application

This application is a Point of Sale (POS) system designed to manage products, sales, and inventory.

## Camera Usage

The application uses the device camera only to scan QR codes of products.

No photos or videos are stored, saved, or uploaded to any server.

## Data Storage

All data such as products, sales, and inventory is stored locally on the user’s device using a local SQLite database.

The application does not upload or share this data with any external server.

## File Export (Database Backup)

The app allows users to export their database (SQLite backup) to the user’s selected location on their device.

The app does not access or read personal files outside of what the user selects.

## Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising data is collected.

## Permissions

The app may request:

* Camera access (for QR scanning only)

No other permissions are used for data collection purposes.

## Third Parties

This application does not share data with any third-party services.

## Contact

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer.
